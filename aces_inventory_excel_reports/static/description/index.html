Customized Inventory report:

With the help of Customized inventory reports, get every bit of information about your inventory in a few seconds. On Odoo, Install the Customized inventory app to acquire information regarding your inventory valuation at different dates and times. Develop inventory reports, forecasted inventory, product moves, stock moves, and inventory valuation reports on a single app. The app allows you to be updated about all the data regarding inventory by letting to produce reports. This report-creating application keeps things uncomplicated to manage.

Features:

  • All in One Reports Creation

    • Get to know the situations regarding your inventory at numerous timings.

  • Get meaningful information about your inventory

    • Save your valuable time and get point-to-point details on a single report document.
