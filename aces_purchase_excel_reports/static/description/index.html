Customized Purchase Report:

Get a permanent solution for creating purchase reports with the Odoo Purchase Report ! With multiple reports on a single module, deliver the customers with appropriately maintained and customized reports. It provides a clear outline or visualization of your purchase cycle by developing custom reports to show your billing status!

Features:

  • Decent Overview of Billings:

    • Check differentiated billing standings on reports.

  • Customized Purchase Report(s)

    • Get multiple customized reports of status billings including RFQ, RFQ sent, to approve, Purchase Orders, Locked and Cancelled orders.

  • Precise and Complete Reports

    • Get Exact and point-to-point details regarding the Purchases of your business!
